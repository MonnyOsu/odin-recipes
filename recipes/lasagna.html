Ingredients

  • Lasagna Sheets
  • 500g Mince
  • Onion
  • Celery
  • Carrot
  • Garlic
  • Can of Tomatos
  • Tomato Paste
  • Beef Stock
  • Cheese
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Butter

Method

  1. Dice Onion, Carrot, Celery and Garlic
  2. Saute in 2tbs of olive oil
  3. Add mince and brown
  4. Add tomato paste
  5. Add can of tomatos and beef stock, season with salt and pepper
  6. Simmer until meat sauce has reduced and thickened
  7. Melt butter in a pot and add an equal amount of flour. Stir in flour until you have a paste
  8. Add 200ml of milk and stir until thickened. Repeat this step until you have your desired bechemel consistency
  9. Add cheese and melt into bechamel
  10. Assemble Lasagna and bake
