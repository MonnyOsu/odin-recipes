Cacio e Pepe

Description

Pasta with butter, cheese and pepper

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Parmesan
  • Ground Black Pepper
  • Spaghetti

Method

  1. Boil water
  2. Add pasta and cook to al dente
  3. While pasta is cooking melt butter in a pan on a low heat
  4. Add ground black pepper to melted butter
  5. Cook until lightly browned
  6. When pasta is cooked add to pan, and reserve some pasta water
  7. add grated parmesan to spaghetti and loosen sauce with pasta water until you have a creamy consitency
